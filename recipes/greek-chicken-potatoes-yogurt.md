Bone-in chicken thighs marinated in lemon, garlic and oregano, then nestled into yellow potatoes that braise in chicken broth until melt-in-your-mouth. One pan, finished with kalamatas and a quick garlicky yogurt sauce on the side.

## Ingredients

### Chicken and potatoes
- 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, divided
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
- 1 tsp dried oregano, divided
- ½ tsp dried thyme
- ¼ cup lemon juice, divided
- 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped, divided
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1.5 lb (~700 g) yellow potatoes (Yukon Gold), peeled and cut into 2" chunks
- ¼ cup Kalamata olives
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped, to garnish

### Garlicky yogurt sauce
- ⅓ cup Greek yogurt
- 1 clove garlic, finely grated
- 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- ¼ tsp dried oregano
- ¼ tsp kosher salt
- ⅛ tsp black pepper

## Method

1. Combine the chicken with 1 tbsp olive oil, the rosemary, ½ tsp oregano, thyme, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 3 cloves of the garlic, salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate 1–6 hours.
2. Heat the oven to 425°F / 220°C.
3. Heat the remaining 2 tbsp olive oil in a large oven-safe pan over medium heat. Add the chicken skin-side down and cook undisturbed for 6–8 minutes until deeply golden. Transfer to a plate.
4. Return the pan to medium heat, pour in the chicken broth and scrape up the brown bits. Stir in the potatoes, the remaining 3 cloves of garlic, the mustard, 2 tbsp lemon juice and ½ tsp oregano.
5. Transfer the pan to the oven and roast for about 40 minutes, until the potatoes are golden and just fork-tender.
6. Nestle the chicken back into the potatoes, scatter over the olives, and return to the oven for another ~12 minutes, until the chicken hits 165°F / 74°C internally and the potatoes are fully tender.
7. While the chicken finishes, stir together everything for the yogurt sauce and taste for seasoning.
8. Garnish with parsley and serve straight from the pan with the yogurt sauce alongside.
