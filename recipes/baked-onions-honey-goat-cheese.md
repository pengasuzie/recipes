## Ingredients

- 2–3 medium onions, halved across the equator
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Butter, cut into small cubes
- A few sprigs of thyme
- Black pepper
- Goat cheese (or Gruyère, mozzarella — whatever melts well)
- Honey, to finish
- Flaky sea salt, to finish

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220°C fan. Grease a muffin tin generously with olive oil.
2. Place an onion half cut-side up in each cup. Salt lightly, drizzle with more olive oil, and top each with a small cube of butter.
3. Bake for 40 minutes, brushing the onions with their juices halfway through.
4. Scatter over the thyme leaves and a good crack of black pepper, then top each onion with a piece of cheese. Return to the oven for 5 minutes until the cheese is soft and just starting to colour.
5. Finish with a drizzle of honey and a pinch of flaky salt.
